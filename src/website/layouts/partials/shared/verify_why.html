Why verify?

Security teams and regulators increasingly require evidence that the third-party components in your build haven't been tampered with - and the bar is rising fast.

http4k Enterprise Edition publishes SLSA provenance, CycloneDX SBOMs, signed licence reports and cosign signatures for every artifact. http4k Verify checks them all automatically, before your code compiles - so the assurance is captured at build time rather than chased down at audit time.
